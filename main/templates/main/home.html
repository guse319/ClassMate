Welcome to ClassMate


If it's your first time here, read on for some helpful tips to use this site.

The inspiration for the ClassMate tool is to lend a helping hand to K12 teachers, students, and parents in tackling COVID-19 learning. Our youth deserve the best education we can give them, pandemic or not.

Teachers, students, and parents acrossed the nation have voiced their concerns of being underequipped, underprepared, and understaffed for efficient online learning. Parents who are forced to school their children at home often cannot even afford the time!

Through ClassMate, university students are invited to volunteer their time to tutor and supplement the education of K12 students via online interation both with the students themselves and their teachers.
